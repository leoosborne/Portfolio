About

I make my living as a digital creative and web entrepreneur, building my own tech-enabled businesses or working inside other companies. Early in my career I found out about photoshop – the world largest box of crayons – and was immediately hooked. This path has lead me to many different projects across the country.

  • Began my career as a presentations specialist and graphic designer for investment banks in NYC
  • Learned project management and web design while running a small family construction company on the Big Island, Hawaii
  • Created three startup companies in the live streaming video events space when Netflix was still sending DVDs through the mail (Periscope Web Video®, Bandtasy® and LeoCast® Live Event Streaming)
  • Furthered my project management and design experience by leading a full make-over of a website for a medium sized liberal arts college in Pennsylvania
  • Returned to Kentucky to continue working on LeoCast® Live Event Streaming
  • Co-founded StructuRight®, an Internet of Things (IoT) structural health and environmental wellness monitoring company; “A fitness tracker for buildings!”
